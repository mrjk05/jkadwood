Data Advisory

Unlock the Full Potential of Your Data with Jins Kadwood’s CDO Advisory Services

In today’s data-driven world, businesses need more than just a strong technical foundation—they need strategic leadership to turn data into actionable insights and sustainable growth. I offer specialised Chief Data Officer consulting, helping organisations design, implement, and scale their data infrastructure while maximising the value of their data assets.

CDO Consulting and Advisory Services

With hands-on experience building advanced data platforms, including real-time Customer Data Platforms (CDPs) for over five companies, I provide the expertise to transform your data into a strategic asset.

What I Offer:

1. Data Infrastructure Design & Implementation
I lead the creation of robust, scalable data infrastructure tailored to your business. From data pipelines to storage and warehousing solutions, I ensure your data is securely and efficiently managed.

2. Data Strategy Development
I collaborate with stakeholders to understand your business goals and translate them into a comprehensive data strategy. I ensure your data architecture is aligned with your strategic objectives and future needs.

3. Scalable ETL Process Development
I develop, maintain, and optimise ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes to seamlessly ingest and process data from diverse sources, creating a reliable flow of data insights.

4. Data Governance & Compliance
I implement data governance policies and procedures to ensure the highest standards of data quality, security, and compliance, safeguarding your organisation against regulatory and operational risks.

5. Integration of Advanced Analytics & Insights
I work alongside your software engineers, data scientists, and analysts to build data-driven solutions that enhance your products and services. Together, we can unlock new opportunities by integrating predictive insights into your decision-making process.

6. Mentorship & Leadership in Data Engineering
I foster a culture of learning and innovation by mentoring junior data engineers and establishing best practices for data management, from code quality to testing and documentation.

7. Driving Innovation through Data
I stay ahead of the curve by leveraging the latest in data engineering, cloud computing, and distributed systems. I evaluate emerging technologies and implement solutions that drive your organisation’s digital transformation.

Key Expertise I Bring:

  • Proven track record of designing and implementing complex data infrastructure
  • Expertise in scalable data pipeline development using frameworks like Apache Spark
  • Deep knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and containerisation technologies
  • Extensive experience with both SQL and NoSQL databases, including Redshift, BigQuery, and Kafka
  • Strong leadership and communication skills, guiding cross-functional teams towards data-driven success

Tailored Engagement Models

Whether your organisation needs help on a specific data initiative or ongoing advisory support, I offer flexible engagement models to fit your needs.

  • Project-Based Consulting: Focused support for specific data engineering or strategy initiatives.
  • Time-Based Advisory: Ongoing guidance to meet evolving data needs.
  • Retainer-Based Partnership: Secure dedicated access to my CDO expertise for continuous data optimisation and innovation.

I am committed to turning your data into a true business asset, helping you enhance performance, enable innovation, and achieve strategic growth.

Jins Kadwood

Hello, I'm Jins Kadwood

I'm the Chief Executive Officer at Kadwood Atelier, crafting luxury bespoke suit  experiences for exceptional women. We believe that when women feel confident and empowered in their attire, they can conquer the world.

As an executive with 20 years of experience, I've launched digital ventures, driven revenue growth, and guided countless teams to success. I believe the key lies in harnessing the brain's predictive powers – anticipating the future and shaping our path towards it. This principle informs my work with startups and organisations, where I help cultivate positive expectations, navigate emotional landscapes, and unlock the power of foresight.

You might be wondering how I went from the corporate world to fashion design. Well, it's been quite a journey! I used to spend my days in boardrooms and strategy meetings. But somewhere along the way, I fell in love with the creativity and craftsmanship of Australian fashion.

I realised that our local industry has so much potential - we have incredible talent, unique perspectives, and a rich cultural heritage to draw from. What we needed was to shine a spotlight on these strengths and help our designers reach a global stage.

That's where I found my calling. I'm not here to shake things up or reinvent the wheel. Instead, I'm passionate about nurturing the immense talent we already have in Australia. My goal is to foster growth, support emerging designers, build fashion business leaders and showcase the exceptional quality of Australian fashion to the world.

I believe that with the right support and exposure, Australian fashion can reclaim its place at the top of the global industry. We have a distinctive style that blends laid-back charm with sophisticated elegance - it's time the world took notice.

Through Kadwood Atelier, I'm working to create opportunities for collaboration, mentorship, and international exposure for Australian designers. It's about building a community that uplifts each other and proudly represents our unique Australian perspective in the fashion world.

Let's connect and explore how we can achieve extraordinary things together.
